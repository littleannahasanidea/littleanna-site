Deleted Scene — His Troublemaker Luna

The Night He Almost Said It

He watched her from across the room and said nothing. That was the whole of it — the watching, the silence, the way he had trained himself to look like a man who had no interest in her at all. He was very good at it by now. He had been practicing for two years.

She laughed at something her friend said and he looked away. He had a rule about that. He was not allowed to watch her laugh. Everything else — the way she walked into a room, the way she argued with his brothers, the dangerous little smile she wore when she was about to do something she absolutely should not do — all of that he could survive. The laugh was different. The laugh was the thing that made him forget all his rules entirely.
